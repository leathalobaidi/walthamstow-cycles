About Me

I am Toni Ward. I run Walthamstow Cycles. I sign off every bike. My standard is simple. Make the bike safe. Explain the work. Deliver on time.

Background

I started the business in 2013 in Walthamstow. I began in a small garage near St James Street. I moved to a dedicated workshop as demand grew. I switched to a mobile collect and return model to remove fixed costs and keep prices fair. Same mechanic. Same standard. Better value.

What I Do

I service everyday bikes for real world riding. City. Hybrid. Road. Kids. Cargo by agreement. I handle E bikes for basic service and safety checks where parts are available. If a job needs a specialist, I say so and point you in the right direction.
